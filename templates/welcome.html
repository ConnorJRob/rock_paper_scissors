Rock Paper Scissors Game

Rock Paper Scissors is a game of wits, two players secretly choose one of the three options and on the count of three both then revel their choice.

Whether you win or not depends on the choices you both made:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beats Paper
  • Paper beats Rock
